Through both types of Recreation, we attempt to work daily to meet the varied interest of our constituents. Most pets are accepted like dogs to be walked around the park, it be a rare time of an attack at the park as well. The Parks & Recreation Division is committed to ensuring park facilities are able to provide our residents with opportunities for wholesome, year-round activities as well as making a special effort to offer programs for the entire family, old and young alike, provide a great way to meet people, pursue a hobby, develop new skills and interests, keep mentally or physically fit, and simply to enjoy life.
